Why Web & API Attacks Matter

Modern breaches rarely begin with malware. They start when attackers manipulate requests, bypass authorization, or abuse transaction logic inside APIs and business workflows.

If your platform processes money, sensitive data, or high-impact actions, these paths must be tested manually. That is exactly where we focuses.

How We Work

Every engagement starts by understanding how your product really works — mapping user roles, API interactions, checkout flows, and backend trust boundaries before testing begins.

Our assessments combine threat modeling, hands-on exploitation, chained attack scenarios, fix verification, and executive-level reporting focused on business risk.
