About IDC

The Internet Defence Company (IDC) is dedicated to helping businesses strengthen their cybersecurity. We specialize in penetration testing services for startups and medium-sized companies. We also provide cybersecurity awareness sessions to employees and staff so they can stay safe from online scams and attacks.

Founder’s Note

I am a cybersecurity student from India and have been studying in this industry for almost 3 years now. Throughout my first year in uni, I found it very difficult to even get called for interviews for any cybersecurity related job roles even though I held industry certifications like CEH. I started IDC so that I could use my skills to help businesses find vulnerabilities and at the same time grow my own business in this industry which I'm very passionate about. Hopefully this business grows and becomes succesfull over the coming years building a reputation as an established security testing company.
