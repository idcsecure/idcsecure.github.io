About IDC

The Internet Defence Company (IDC) is here to make cybersecurity simple and reliable for businesses that are building their future. We focus on helping startups and growing companies stay safe as they expand in today’s connected world.

Our core service is penetration testing — a proactive way to spot weaknesses in websites and networks before attackers do. We keep things straightforward, delivering clear insights and practical recommendations that make sense for your business.

But we don’t stop at technology. People are just as important, which is why we also run cybersecurity awareness sessions for teams. These sessions equip employees with the knowledge to recognize scams, avoid online threats, and build everyday security habits.

Why Choose us?

  • Focused on Small Businesses – We understand the needs of startups and growing companies, keeping our services practical and affordable.
  • Clear and Actionable Reports – No confusing jargon. We provide straightforward findings and simple steps to fix them.
  • People-First Approach – Along with testing, we train your team to spot scams and avoid online risks.
  • Trusted Partnership – We work with you, not just for you — making sure your business grows with confidence.
