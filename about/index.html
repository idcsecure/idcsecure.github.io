About IDC

The Internet Defense Company is a cybersecurity consultancy and service provider dedicated to empowering businesses with robust, real-world security solutions.

IDC offers a suite of modern services—from vulnerability scans and penetration testing to phishing and dark web campaigns, custom security policy development and brand protection.

We deliver value through actionable assessments, practical training, and custom solutions tailored to your business' security needs. Whether it’s hardening your network, training your team to resist phishing, or protecting your online reputation, IDC is your trusted partner for cybersecurity resilience and growth.

Why Choose us?

  • Focused on Small Businesses – We understand the needs of startups and growing companies, keeping our services practical and affordable.
  • Clear and Actionable Reports – No confusing jargon. We provide straightforward findings and simple steps to fix them.
  • People-First Approach – Along with testing, we train your team to spot scams and avoid online risks.
  • Trusted Partnership – We work with you, not just for you — making sure your business grows with confidence.
